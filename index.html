Nina The Clownfish

Species: Ocellaris

length: 11 cm (4.3 inches)

Feeding and Foods:

  • Feed adults twice a day and juveniles 3 to 4 times a day
  • Crustaceans (such as copepods and amphipods)
  • Algae
  • Polychaete Worms
  • Leftovers from the anemone's meal

Eartha The Freshwater African Butterflyfish

Species: Buchholzi

Length: 12.95 cm (5.1 inches)

Feeding and Foods:

  • Several feedings per day
  • Feed flies
  • Mosquito Larvae
  • Small Spiders
  • Worms
  • Small Fish
  • Brine Shrimp

Josephine The Betta

Species: Splendens

Length: 7.01 cm (2.8 in)

Feeding and Foods:

  • Generally feed once or twice a day
  • Zooplankton
  • Insect Larvae
  • Crustaceans
  • Aquatic Insects
  • Brine Shrimp
  • Blood Worms

Zora The Seahorse

Species: Hippocampus spp

Length: 5 to 36 cm (2 to 14 in)

Feeding and Foods:

  • Feed 2-3 times daily
  • Plankton
  • Fish Larvae
